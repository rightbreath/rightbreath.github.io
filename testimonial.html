Torna fort - My first trekking Experience This was my first and long awaited trek. The idea of going to trekking was first suggested to me by my friend,Darshan Gugle. At first I was not easily convinced for going to a trek as I had never done it before. He assured me that this is a small trek and u will not find any difficulty in trekking . Feeling confident by Darshan's words , I agreed as I needed a break and wanted to relax in the lap of nature. Our trekking destination was fixed , Torna Fort (Prachand gad) . We did small preparation in early mrng for trek and packed our bags with essential items. Finnaly after long wait, we left, It took 1 nd half hr. to reach this village which is base of Torna fort. As soon we reached Velhe, we had breakfast in the local eatery & started our Trek at 10.45 am. As we started trekking, I found it difficult and little scary at the beginning as we are at bottom level. We climbed a lot of steep slopes and I was out of breath as I had never climbed a fort before but Will power was boosted by other member of group. Meanwhile nature mesmerized me. We took one 15 min long break on half way & reached at 12.45 Pm on the fort. As soon as we reached at the top and Cameras came out of the bags , posers went crazy and bas photo,photo aur photo.
