About Us

Trekfit group is a group basically started to encourage trekking among people.Many people love travelling,trekking but cant do it due to the lesser known information about the treks or lesser known professional groups.Trekfit group basically organises treks in Sahyadri mountains,Nature Introduction camps,some adventure sports like rock climbing,rappeling.Encouraging fitness among people,nature love and create an overall good personality among trekkers with the increase in will power,confidence,happiness is what we provide. Expert Trekkers,nature experts run all these activities.They have travelled far and wide in the mountains of Sahyadri.We will start with Himalaya treks soon. We are unique in organising treks as we see to it that complete information about the trek alongwith its history is given to the participants,group activities are taken,some group games/sports are played on the top,we see to it that everyone participates in the group activities. We promise to take you to new places,some unexplored forts through some thick jungles of and misleading trails of Sahyadris. So tie ur shoe laces, buckle up,puts ur straps on,warm up and be ready to explore the nature with Trekfit group. We know how important weekends are.Lets enjoy the weekends in a different way.Why waste money partying when you can enjoy being fit and exolore a new place altogether.The adventure thrill will surely be something different and addictive for all of u.Safety of all the trekkers matters the most and hence we see to it that we carry all the safety equipments along with us.Care and satisfaction of each and every trekker is our main motto.
