Frequently Asked Questions


1.What is the schedule of treks?

- Shorter treks of 1 or 2 days will be scheduled on weekends mostly Sunday.The schedule of longer treks will be decided as per availabilities.


2.When would I be informed about the treks?

- For shorter treks 3-5 days in advance.For longer treks the schedule will be given well in advance.


3.What do I need to carry with me during treks?

-2L water, a napkin,a towel, a pair of extra clothes. In case of any special need for any trek you will be informed beforehand.


4.How frequently will the treks be organized?

-We will try our best to organize them every weekend.


5.Will I be provided breakfast,lunch/dinner and transport facilities?

-Yes, the organization will take care of the above. For any special need you will be informed in advance.


6.What is the payment method and when should I pay?

-You must pay before the day of the trek to confirm your participation. Payment will be done through online payment method. Details for the same will be available shortly


7.From where should I board the bus?

-The team will meet at a central location convenient for all which will be decided and will be informed to all.


For any further queries please visit the Contact Us page.
